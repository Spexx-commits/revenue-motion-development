# Week 11–14: Scale Readiness Sprint

Stage: Scale Readiness  
Owner: Founder  
Duration: 4 Weeks  
Objective: Make explicit strategic commitments and build infrastructure to scale a validated revenue motion.

Stage 4 is not discovery.
It is commitment.

You are preparing the company for scale.

---

# Overview

During this sprint you will:

1. Make explicit ICP strategy decisions
2. Finalize channel scaling plan
3. Design organizational structure
4. Build infrastructure and playbooks
5. Model 12-month scale plan

You are no longer asking “Does this work?”

You are asking “How do we scale what works?”

---

# Week 11: ICP Strategy Decision

Choose one:

Primary ICP Focus  
- Concentrate all resources on highest-performing segment

Multi-ICP Strategy  
- Maintain multiple segments with defined resource allocation

Decision Criteria:
- Unit economics by segment
- Sales cycle length
- Channel scalability
- Organizational complexity

Deliverable:
- Written ICP strategy
- Explicit deprioritization of non-core segments

---

# Week 12: Channel Scaling Plan

Classify channels:

Tier 1 – Scale aggressively  
Tier 2 – Maintain opportunistically  
Tier 3 – Eliminate  

Define:
- Budget allocation
- Headcount allocation
- Target pipeline contribution per channel

Deliverable:
- Channel scaling roadmap
- Resource allocation plan

---

# Week 13: Organizational Design

Define:

- When specialization begins (SDR vs AE)
- Hiring profiles
- Span of control
- Leadership capability requirements

Clarify:
- What first revenue leader must own
- What they are NOT expected to discover

Deliverable:
- Organizational design doc
- Hiring scorecards

---

# Week 14: Infrastructure & Scale Modeling

Build:

- Complete sales playbooks
- 30/60/90 onboarding plan
- CRM configuration
- Forecasting methodology
- Compensation model aligned with validated metrics

Model:

- 12-month hiring plan
- Ramp time assumptions
- Pipeline coverage requirements
- Capital efficiency projections

Deliverable:
- Scale readiness report
- Go / No-Go decision for hiring

---

# Exit Criteria

You may hire and scale only if:

- ICP strategy is explicit
- Tier 1 channel identified
- Fully-loaded CAC validated
- Sales cycle stable
- Playbooks documented
- Hiring model stress-tested

If any of these are unclear, delay hiring.
